Verbs

This section contains information about the different Sanskrit verbs. More specifically, it contains all of the different conjugations that you're likely to encounter in your studies. These conjugations are split into two broad groups:

  • the simple verb classes (a+, a0, ya, aya)
  • the complex verb classes (consonant, nā, no, o, na, duplication)

For information about prefixes and secondary verb forms, please see the corresponding lessons. I hope to list that information here in the future.
